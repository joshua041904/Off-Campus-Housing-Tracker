Messaging rate limiting + spam + recommendation
-------------------------------------------------
- docs/MESSAGING_RATE_LIMIT_AND_SPAM.md: Redis rate limit (key rate:msg:{user_id}, INCR, EXPIRE 60s; max 30/min, 500/day); do NOT use Kafka for rate limiting. Trust spam: consume MessageSentV1, user_spam_score, emit UserSuspendedV1; messaging rejects SendMessage for suspended users. Recommendation: Analytics read model user_listing_engagement from MessageSentV1 + booking + views; signals and ranking inputs (tenant/landlord side).
- infra/db/05-trust-spam-score.sql: trust.user_spam_score (user_id, score, updated_at).
- scripts/ensure-trust-schema.sh: apply 05-trust-spam-score.sql after 04.
