Booking state machine (DB-enforced)
-----------------------------------
- infra/db/02-booking-state-machine.sql: trigger booking.enforce_booking_transition()
  on booking.bookings. Legal: created → pending_confirmation | cancelled;
  pending_confirmation → confirmed | rejected | cancelled | expired;
  confirmed → completed | cancelled. Terminal states (rejected, cancelled, expired,
  completed) allow no further transitions; trigger raises on illegal transition.

Trust scoring (deterministic, event-driven)
-------------------------------------------
- infra/db/02-trust-scoring.sql: reputation_score as NUMERIC(4,2) 0–5. Formula
  trust.compute_reputation_score(positive, negative, completed_bookings, flags):
  average_rating*0.6 + completed_bookings*0.2 - flags_count*0.3, LEAST(GREATEST(...,0),5).
  Trigger recomputes on insert/update of reputation; backfill for existing rows.
