Summary
-------
- Auth DB carries two complementary outbox surfaces: Prisma migration table
  auth.auth_outbox (publisher + account-deletion flow) and infra/db
  auth.outbox_events (proto-style transactional outbox). Inspect and bootstrap
  checks now treat both as first-class so schema reports and verify-bootstrap
  match real OCH restores instead of failing on naming alone.

  auth.outbox_events and auth.auth_outbox (updated comments).
