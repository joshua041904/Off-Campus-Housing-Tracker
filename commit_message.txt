fix(observability,edge,analytics): restore subpath routing and full metrics scrape coverage

Align edge and observability runtime behavior so /grafana and /prometheus are
stable under subpaths, and ensure Prometheus scrapes all housing services/exporters.

- Edge routing:
  - Caddy / caddy-h3 ConfigMap keeps full /grafana/* and /prometheus/* paths
    (no strip_prefix), adds explicit no-slash redirects, and forwards tracing/edge headers.
